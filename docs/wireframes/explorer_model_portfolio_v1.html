This is the shipped model-portfolio foundation slice. Equity allocates on two levels (international share of the sleeve, then the domestic cap split); debt allocates on credit by risk tier and duration by horizon. Every tier keeps a non-zero large-cap core and a non-zero, non-dominant international allocation, the never-zero principle.

Tier resolution is stated, not practiced. The tier comes from the investor's stated risk appetite string; a practiced (allocation-derived) classifier does not exist on main and is proposed separately (audit Part B, BM6). All values are house view v1, named and tunable.
