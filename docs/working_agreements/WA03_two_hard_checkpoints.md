# WA03: Two hard checkpoints per workstream

## Agreement

Two hard checkpoints per workstream. Workstream-template-shaped; the specific checkpoints vary per workstream but the pattern of two formal review gates is durable.

## Rationale

Transcribed from CC build prompts; original rationale not in-repo. In-repo evidence: the A2 and risk-reward prompts both define exactly two HARD CHECKPOINTs (audit plus design, then candidate output). The gate forces the owner's taste calls before expensive or hard-to-reverse work (computation code, fixture backfill).

## Trigger

Transcribed from CC build prompts; original rationale not in-repo.

## Examples

**Compliance:** Risk-reward stopped at Hard Checkpoint 1 (audit plus design) and will stop again at Hard Checkpoint 2 (Shailesh candidate stats) before backfill.

**Non-compliance:** Proceeding from the design proposal straight into fixture backfill without presenting candidate stats for approval.

## Cross-references

WA06 (flag and wait freely, the between-checkpoints discipline). Sub-checkpoints (for example the risk-reward ambiguous-fund and pre-recompute-sample stops) are owner-inserted extensions of this pattern.
